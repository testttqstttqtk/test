We're looking for a Senior UX/UI Designer to join our team and help create exceptional digital experiences for our clients. The ideal candidate will have a strong portfolio demonstrating their ability to solve complex design challenges and create intuitive, visually appealing interfaces.

Responsibilities:

  • Lead the UX/UI design process for client projects from concept to implementation
  • Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs for websites and applications
  • Conduct user research and usability testing to inform design decisions
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams including developers, strategists, and project managers
  • Present design concepts and rationale to clients and internal stakeholders

Requirements:

  • 5+ years of experience in UX/UI design for digital products
  • Strong portfolio demonstrating exceptional design skills and problem-solving abilities
  • Proficiency in design tools such as Figma, Adobe XD, or Sketch
  • Experience with design systems and component-based design
  • Understanding of accessibility standards and best practices
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills
  • Bachelor's degree in Design, HCI, or related field (or equivalent experience)

How to Apply

Application Process

01

Apply Online

Browse our open positions and submit your application through our online portal. Include your resume, portfolio (if applicable), and a cover letter explaining why you're interested in joining Aramana.

02

Initial Review

Our hiring team will review your application and assess your qualifications against the position requirements. If there's a potential match, we'll reach out to schedule an initial conversation.

03

Interviews

The interview process typically includes a phone screening, followed by 1-2 interviews with team members and leadership. For creative and technical roles, we may also include a skills assessment or portfolio review.

04

Offer & Onboarding

Successful candidates will receive an offer letter outlining compensation and benefits. Once accepted, our HR team will guide you through the onboarding process to ensure a smooth transition to your new role.

No open positions that fit?

Send us your resume anyway
